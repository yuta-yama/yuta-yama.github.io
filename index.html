Yuta Yamaguchi


Yuta's filmography includes movies that have screened at the Cannes Film Festival in 2010, 2011, and 2014. Yuta was lauded by Indiewire for his, “striking visual work” on Wolf, which premiered at SXSW and won the Panavision Best Texas Feature Film Award at the Dallas International Film Festival.

Yuta also served as the Associate Producer on August Evening, which won the John Cassavetes Award at the 2008 Independent Spirit Awards. Bilingual in English and Japanese, Yuta is a graduate of the Radio-Television-Film program at the University of Texas.
